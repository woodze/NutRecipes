    • 2. Spaghetti with Sun-Dried-Tomato-Almond Pesto

    Ingredients

  • 1/2 c. drained oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1/3 c. salted roasted almonds
  • 1 large clove garlic
  • 1/2 c. extra-virgin olive oil
  • salt
  • freshly ground pepper
  • 1/2 c. fresh bread crumbs
  • 12 oz. spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp. copped parsley

    • Instructions

  • In a food processor, pulse the tomatoes, almonds, and garlic. Add 1/4 cup of the oil and puree. Season with salt and pepper.
  • In a skillet, toast the bread crumbs in 2 tablespoons of the oil, stirring, until golden. Transfer the crumbs to a plate; season with salt and pepper.
  • In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the pasta until al dente. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of the cooking liquid. In the pot, toss the pasta with the pesto, reserved cooking water, and remaining 2 tablespoons of oil until the sauce clings to the pasta. Transfer the pasta to bowls, sprinkle with the bread crumbs and parsley, and drizzle with olive oil.
